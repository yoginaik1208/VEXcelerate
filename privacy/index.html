Privacy Policy for VEXcelerate

Last updated: October 7, 2025

Introduction

VEXcelerate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our VEX IQ robotics tools application.

Information We Collect

Local Data Only: VEXcelerate stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The app may store locally:

  • Team scores and performance data you enter
  • Timer settings and preferences
  • Calculator results and match data
  • App settings and preferences

How We Use Information

All data remains on your device and is used solely to:

  • Provide VEX IQ scoring calculations
  • Track team performance over time
  • Save your app preferences and settings
  • Enable offline functionality

Data Sharing

We do not share any data. Since all information is stored locally on your device, we have no access to your data and do not share it with third parties.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend keeping your device secure with appropriate locks and access controls.

Children's Privacy

VEXcelerate is designed for VEX IQ participants, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
  • Modify: You can edit or delete data within the app

Third-Party Services

VEXcelerate does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

VEX IQ Compliance

VEXcelerate uses official VEX IQ scoring rules and is designed to support the VEX IQ robotics program. We are not officially affiliated with VEX Robotics.
